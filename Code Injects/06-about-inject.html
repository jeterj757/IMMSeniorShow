Design + Technology

Interactive Multimedia at TCNJ connects design, storytelling, production, and technology. Senior Show is where those skills meet a live audience: playable systems, visual stories, fabricated objects, installations, prototypes, and experiments built to be experienced.

The work is varied by design. Some projects invite play, some ask for reflection, and some show the craft behind a tool, scene, or system. Six specializations. One night. Walk through all of them.
