Solid concept — the signature field is genuinely personal and different from every other senior show poster. These six changes make it production-ready without losing what makes it yours.
Fix Logo
The original typed "IMM" + "Senior Showcase" in two different styles fights itself. Replaced with WhiteExponentialLogo.svg — one unified mark that already has both lines locked together with the right weight relationship.
Fix Signatures
Names were same-weight as the event info — made it hard to read. Moved signatures to a lighter layer (0.50–0.82 opacity, fading toward the center text zone). They read as atmosphere, not noise.
Fix Date presentation
The emoji pin + clock icons read as casual. Replaced with a frosted glass info chip — same date and location, but typographically consistent with the rest of the brand.
Fix Location
"AIMM" alone means nothing to anyone who hasn't been to campus. Full name: AIMM · The College of New Jersey · Ewing, NJ.
Add Free & Open to the Public
Was missing entirely. This removes the single biggest reason people don't show up — they assume they need a student ID or a ticket.
Keep Mountain backdrop + all 37 signatures
The illustrated landscape and the signed names are what make this design actually yours. Every student's name is on the poster — that alone gets people sharing it. Keep both.
