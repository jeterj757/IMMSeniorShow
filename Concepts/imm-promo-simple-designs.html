Simple design directions

Built from the current IMM style and the flyer references.

The goal here is not to restart the look. It is to simplify it: keep the postcard frame, keep the scenic background, keep the bold IMM mark, and turn each deliverable into a clean, readable version of the same system.

Main Graphic

Keep the main graphic as a simple postcard composition: scenic background, a tilted card, the IMM logo centered big, and one date block. Let the logo do the work and avoid packing it with extra copy.

Use this same structure for the hero image, social banner, poster, and stage screen opener.

Motion Graphic

Animate the current design, not a new one. The card can slide in, tilt slightly, and settle while clouds, birds, and background layers drift.
